In all operations related to the expenditure of funds of general obligation bonds approved by the voters under proposition 39, the district shall follow the requirements set forth in Education Code 15264 et seq. ("Strict Accountability in Local School Construction Bond Act of 2000").

In keeping with these requirements, the district shall maintain procedures for establishing and maintaining an appropriately constituted and independent Citizens' Oversight Committee.
