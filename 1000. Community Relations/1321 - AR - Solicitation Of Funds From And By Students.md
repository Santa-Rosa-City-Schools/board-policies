The Board of Education recognizes the need for students to occasionally conduct fund-raising activities in order to provide for the maintenance and enrichment of student activity programs. Such activities should be conducted in a manner which will provide the funding necessary without frequent broad and direct solicitation of the public and community. No student shall be required to raise a specific amount of money in order to participate in an activity sponsored by a school related organization.
