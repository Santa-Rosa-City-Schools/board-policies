ATTESTATION REGARDING TITLE I PARAPROFESSIONALSSchool: ____________ Principal: __________
1.
I certify that every paraprofessional hired after January 8, 2002, and working in a program supported
by Title I funds, unless he/she is exempted by law, has received a high school diploma or its equivalent
and has met at least one of the following criteria pursuant to 20 USC
6319
:
a.
Completed at least two years of study at an institution of higher education
b.
Obtained an associate's or higher degree
c.
Met a rigorous standard of quality and demonstrated, through a locally determined academic
assessment, knowledge of and the ability to assist in instructing either reading, writing and
mathematics or reading readiness, writing readiness and mathematics readiness
2.
All paraprofessionals working in a Title I program are performing duties consistent with 20 USC
6319
.
