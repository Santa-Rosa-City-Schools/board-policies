Administrators and educational partners use the Curriculum Project Approval Form to obtain approvalfor projects. All sections of the form must contain complete information.The Curriculum Project Approval Form must be submitted to the Office of Curriculum and Instruction,
7-12, a minimum of six weeks prior to the anticipated start date of the project, in order to be presented
to the Board of Education for approval.
