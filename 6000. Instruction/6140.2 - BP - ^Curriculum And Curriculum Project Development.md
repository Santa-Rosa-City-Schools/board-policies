Santa Rosa City Schools will provide an instructional program for all students which will provide for full realization of individual capacities, the improvement of human relationships, the development of civic and social responsibility, and economic productivity and fulfills all requirements of law.

Curriculum development involves democratic procedures and a cooperative approach, with participation by students, teachers, administrators, educational partners and members of the community; experimentation in the use of new methods and materials, and study of problems by task forces on the recommendation of the appropriate councils. Resources include production of courses of study, teaching guides, curriculum projects, and instructional materials by district and local school staffs, consultant services, and continuous communication with school personnel and community.
